About this graph:
Efficiency frontier for trade-off between Economic gain (total gross margin €) and Ecological Functions index (mean of three normalized functions: n-Semi-natural grasslands, n-Pollinator community size, and n-Pollination service).

Each point represents one of 50,000 simulations. Color shows grassy field margin width. Bar charts show function contributions. Below are maps reflecting landscape features of solution regions (see Table 1).
